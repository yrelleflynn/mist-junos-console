CHECK: DHCP Lease
ID: dhcp-lease
File: src/checks/dhcp-lease.check.ts

PURPOSE
Checks whether the switch has an active DHCP lease on its management
interface. Confirms the lease is bound (not expired or in a pending
state) and reports the server IP, lease time, and interface.

COMMANDS
  show dhcp client binding

PASS
  At least one DHCP binding is shown in BOUND state. Server IP and
  remaining lease time are reported.

WARN
  A binding exists but is in REQUESTING, RENEWING, or REBINDING state.
  The switch has a lease but may lose it soon.

FAIL
  No bindings found, or all bindings are in INIT or EXPIRED state.

SKIP
  The management interface is statically addressed (no DHCP client
  configured), or DHCP client process is not running.

REMEDIATION
  - Run: restart dhcp-local-server — restart the DHCP client.
  - Confirm the management VLAN reaches a DHCP server.
  - Run: show log dhclient — inspect DHCP client log for errors.
  - If DHCP server is Mist-controlled, check site DHCP settings in the
    Mist dashboard.
